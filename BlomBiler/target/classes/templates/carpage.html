Ferrari 296 GTB

The Ferrari 296 GTB is a hybrid sports car produced by the Italian automaker Ferrari. It was introduced in 2021 as a replacement for the Ferrari F8 Tributo. The car is powered by a 2.9-liter V6 engine coupled with an electric motor, which together produce a total output of 818 horsepower. The car can accelerate from 0 to 60 mph in just 2.9 seconds and has a top speed of over 205 mph.
